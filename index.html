WE OFFER

3D Animation, Modeling and
GAME DEVELOPMENT

ZLENDER is a creative 3D studio delivering stylized cartoon animation, high quality 3D modeling, and complete game development. Our Team provides services from character and environment creation to animation and interactive gameplay.

3D Animation Videos

Our 3D animation services focus on creating expressive cartoon animations, cinematic sequences, and character-driven content designed to engage and tell strong visual stories. From storyboards and animatics to final renders, we handle the full animation pipeline with clarity and creative direction.

Whether you're producing animated shorts, brand characters, or narrative-driven content, we bring movement, personality, and polish to every frame — delivering animations that are visually consistent, emotionally engaging, and ready for production use across multiple platforms.

Game Development

Our game development services cover stylized and immersive experiences across indie, casual, and mid-core projects for PC, mobile, and cross-platform builds. We support your vision from early concept to final release, delivering custom gameplay systems, polished mechanics, and optimized builds tailored to your platform and production goals.

Working with modern engines and efficient pipelines, our developers and artists collaborate closely to prototype quickly, iterate intelligently, and refine gameplay without sacrificing performance or visual quality.

Modeling & Design

Our 3D modeling services deliver high-quality, production-ready assets including characters, props, and environments built for animation, games, and interactive experiences. We focus on clean topology, optimized geometry, and detailed textures to ensure assets integrate into your pipeline.

From stylized cartoon models to detailed world-building assets, we create flexible 3D content that is ready for rigging, animation, or real-time engines — helping you build rich, consistent worlds with efficiency and precision.
